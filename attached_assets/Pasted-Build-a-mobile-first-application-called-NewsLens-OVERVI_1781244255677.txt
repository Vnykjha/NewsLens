Build a mobile-first application called "NewsLens".

OVERVIEW

NewsLens is an AI-native news intelligence platform that helps users understand a news story rather than simply consume it. The app acts as a "second opinion" for every article by providing credibility analysis, multiple perspectives, contextual summaries, citations, and community-driven discussion.

The primary goal is to help users answer:

* Is this article credible?
* What are other sources saying?
* What context am I missing?
* What happened before this?
* Is any media being manipulated?
* What do other readers think?

DESIGN STYLE

DO NOT create a modern SaaS dashboard.

DO NOT use dark themes, glowing gradients, cyberpunk styling, glassmorphism, coding aesthetics, terminal-like UIs, or futuristic AI visuals.

The design should feel like:

* A modern newspaper
* A magazine
* Substack
* Medium
* Instagram-level usability

Visual Style:

* White background
* Light grey cards
* Black typography
* Newspaper-inspired headings
* Serif fonts for article titles
* Sans-serif fonts for body text
* High readability
* Rounded but subtle corners
* Minimal clutter
* Clean spacing

The experience should feel:

* Trustworthy
* Editorial
* Human
* Simple
* Fast

MULTI-PERSPECTIVE ANALYSIS

For every article display:

Supporting Coverage:
Articles reaching similar conclusions.

Alternative Perspectives:
Different framing and interpretations.

Contradictory Coverage:
Sources challenging the claims.

Users should immediately understand:
"The other side of the story."

CREDIBILITY ANALYSIS

Never label content as simply True or False.

Instead show:

Credibility Score

Source Reputation

Evidence Strength

Cross-Source Verification

Missing Evidence

Potential Bias Indicators

Output example:

Credibility Score: 82/100

Reasons:

* Multiple independent sources
* Supporting evidence found
* Contradictory reporting available
